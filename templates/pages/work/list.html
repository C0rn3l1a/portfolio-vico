Hoyts Self Serve Kiosk

My final dissertation I did as part of my Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design. I focused on redesigning the Cine Hoyts digital kiosk interface — a touchscreen platform for movie ticket and food purchases. Through research, analysis, and a user-centered design approach, the goal was to improve usability, visual organization, and overall user experience.
